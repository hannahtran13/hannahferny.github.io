About me!

I’m a senior at Bucknell University studying Computer Science and Environmental Studies. My work focuses on translating complex systems-especially in sustainability and technology-into clear, engaging experiences. I’ve worked across marketing, research, and design, building campaigns, analyzing audience behavior, and creating narratives that connect technical ideas with real people.

Recently, I’ve:
• Designed cross-platform marketing campaigns for public-facing environmental programs
• Led research on social media narratives and audience perception
• Built exhibitions and digital experiences that make research accessible

I’m especially interested in work that combines AI, storytelling, and sustainability, not just to inform people, but to change how they think and act.
